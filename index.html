The Towering Story

WELCOME TO TOWERING TOTS SCHOOL

We are desirous to ensure we provide the best education possible to our Learners. Management, Staff and Learners are extremely grateful of our achievements so far without being complacent. We look to improve and move forward.

I hope you enjoy browsing through our website. Should you require further information please contact the School. Parents and the community are encouraged to stay involved in their children’s education, and join us as we strive to make each child “Nothing but the Best”.

Mrs. Adedolapo Vaatia
Head of School

Established January 10, 1983

Founded by Mrs. A.A Vaatia, Towering Tots began with a modest team of 5 and has grown into a prestigious institution catering to over 1100 pupils.

Our Vision

To create an innovative, inspiring learning community where children, teachers, and parents learn alongside each other in a safe environment.

Our Mission

To inspire children to grip the challenges of learning, equipping them with the skills, confidence, and determination to succeed.

Where Magic Meets Curriculum

We’re About The Kids…

CLASSES

Starting school is an exciting time for your child. Our schools provide a rich, conducive and friendly learning environment and state of the art facilities for all children to learn and grow from the preparatory class to primary class.

PRACTICAL LEARNING

We do our best in creating a conducive learning environment complete with standard classrooms, science laboratory, well equipped resource centre that encourages pupils to learn independently, computer room and playgrounds for our pupils.

PARENTS WELCOME

We understand that the adjustment to School is a major change for both Parent and Child. Hence we involve the Parents in our process by creating avenues for the Parents and Teachers to come together for the educational development of the Learners.

EVENTS

The school calendar gives room for extracurricular activities like excursions, inter-school quizzes and inter-house competitions to mention but a few aimed at ensuring our pupils educational experience and development physically and socially.

Kiddie Corner

The Houses

Sparking healthy competition and unity through our four houses: Ade-ifere, Dove, Gema, and Palm. Whether it's sports or debating, every Tot belongs to a family.

Clubs & Hobbies

French, Football, Singing, Science, Art, Music, Computer, and even Philately! Our clubs run throughout the year, helping learners discover their unique potential.

Family Rapport

Through the P.T.A, Caring Mothers Forum, and regular Open Days, parents are active partners in their children's educational journey.
